# Milestone 1 Notes

## Domain

My project domain is Georgia Tech CS course and professor reviews / student advice.

This knowledge is valuable because official course catalogs describe requirements and topics, but they usually do not explain what students actually experience: workload, exam style, professor teaching quality, project difficulty, and practical advice. This information is scattered across Reddit threads, review sites, and informal student discussions, so a RAG system can make it easier to search and answer questions from collected student comments.

## Documents

I collected 10 text documents in `data/raw/`. These documents are curated source briefs based on public student discussions about Georgia Tech CS courses, workload, professor/course experiences, and course planning advice.

## Example questions this system should answer

1. What do students say about CS 3510 workload?
2. What kind of difficulty do students mention for CS 1332?
3. What do students say about CS 2340 projects?
4. Which courses are described as heavy or time-consuming?
5. What advice do students give for systems courses like CS 3210 or CS 3220?
